Sourdough

Description

Sourdough bread is a staple of any cottage-core home and it's actually quite simple to make. Anyone can do it!

Ingredients

  • Sourdough Starter
  • Bread Flour
  • Water
  • Salt

Recipie

  1. Mix the starter with the water, salt, and flour
  2. Allow to rise for several hours, kneading occasionally
  3. Bake the bread at 450 for an hour
