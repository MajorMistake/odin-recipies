Chicken Alfredo

Description

This recipie is an italian-american classic and one of my favorites. It's one of the first things I learned to make myself and today, I'll be going over the basics on how to make your alfredo shine!


Ingredients

  • Heavy Cream
  • Butter
  • Parmesian Cheese
  • Pasta
  • Chicken Breasts
  • Spices

Recipie

  1. Season and grill the chicken breasts, then slice thinly
  2. Cook pasta according to package instructions and then strain
  3. Melt the butter in a saucepan, then add the heavy cream and parmesian
  4. Combine all items into the saucepan and enjoy!
