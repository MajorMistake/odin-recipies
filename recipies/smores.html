S'Mores

Description

Who doesn't love the taste of a fresh s'more, fresh off the campfire? You can make s'mores of your own with just three simple ingredients!

Ingredients

  • Graham Crackers
  • Chocolate Bar
  • Marshmallows

Recipie

  1. Assemble two graham crackers, both with a few squares of chocolate on top
  2. Toast a marshmallow or two over an open flame, until golden
  3. Spread the gooey marshmallows over one of the graham cracker stacks and smush the other stack on top to complete your treat!
